# Privacy

This is a static site that exists to help impacted Aeroseal alumni find their next role. We take privacy seriously because the people listed here deserve dignity and control.

## What we publish

Only what each person explicitly submitted and approved: name, headline, location, role history at Aeroseal, skills, project highlights, peer kudos, a "looking for" statement, and links they chose to share. Optional: a photo, a resume PDF, a manager note.

We do **not** publish:
- Email addresses or phone numbers (a contact relay handles inbound messages)
- Salary or compensation data
- Aeroseal-internal documents, codenames, or confidential details
- Anything submitted without the subject's explicit opt-in

## How "contact this person" works

Messages route through a third-party relay (Formspree) and are reviewed by the maintainer before being forwarded to the profile owner. No alum email addresses are stored in the public site. The contact button collects the sender's name, email, and message — nothing else. The maintainer's forward is manual, which adds a layer of review before any recruiter message reaches a listed alum.

## Cookies and tracking

None set by this site. Vercel Analytics (privacy-friendly, no personal data, no cookies) records page-view counts in aggregate so we know whether the site is being used. No third-party trackers, no advertising pixels.

## How to be removed

Message the maintainer. Your `.mdx` file is deleted and the site is redeployed, typically within 24 hours. After redeploy:
- The profile page returns 404.
- The profile is removed from the landing grid and search index.
- Cached copies on third-party services (Google, archive.org) are outside our control; we'll request removal from Google's index on request.

## Marking yourself placed

You decide when. Message the maintainer and we'll flip the flag. We do not infer placement from LinkedIn changes or other sources.

## Site sunset

The site is intended to operate for one year from public launch. After that, we'll archive the repo and either take the site down or leave a static "alumni who landed" page up, depending on what makes sense. Profiles can be removed earlier on request — see above.
